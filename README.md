## 👥 Who Can Use This Portfolio?

This portfolio template is designed for a wide range of users:

- 🎓 **Students** – Showcase academic projects, skills, and learning journey.
- 💼 **Professionals** – Present experience, work history, and achievements in a modern way.
- 🆕 **Beginners / New Developers** – Start building your online presence with an easy-to-customize template.
- 🧑‍💻 **Freelancers** – Attract clients by showcasing your work and services.
- 🚀 **Tech Enthusiasts** – Build and personalize your own portfolio with modern technologies.
- 🏢 **Job Seekers** – Create a strong digital resume to stand out to recruiters.

> 💡 Anyone can use this portfolio to build a strong personal brand and showcase their skills online.
